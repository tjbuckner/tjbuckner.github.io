Welcome
Highway Markings, Inc. specializes in the application of reflectorized paint, thermoplastic, tape, and polyurea roadway markings. We also install snowplowable reflective markers, raised reflective markers, and reflectorized road signs. Our hydroblasting marking removal system with vaccuum recovery provides fast and clean results on asphalt or concrete. We also provide new installation of steel, wood, and decorative guardrail; we also offer complete repair solutions for all highway guardrail systems.

Our knoweldgeable staff will help find the right solution for your roadway safety needs; and if we can't we will find you someone who can.
